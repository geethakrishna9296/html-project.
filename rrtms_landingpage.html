About


Property maintenance involves the upkeep of a building or property to ensure it remains in good condition and meets safety and functional standards. This applies to residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Proper maintenance helps prevent deterioration, reduces the risk of costly repairs, and enhances the property's value. Reservation System :- Implementing a reservation system for property maintenance can help streamline the process, improve efficiency, and ensure that maintenance tasks are scheduled and addressed in a timely manner.
